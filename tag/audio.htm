Audio Elements Overview -- These Audio related components and elements are supported by the HomeDing library.

RDA 5807 FM Radio Tuner -- The RDA5807 is a radio chip for receiving FM stereo radio signals and RDS information.

SI4703 FM Radio Tuner -- The SI4703 created by Silicon Labs and now owned by SkyWorks is a radio chip for receiving FM stereo radio signals and RDS information.

SI47xx FM / Multiband Radio Tuner -- The SI47xx is a series of radio chip from SiliconSilicon Labs, now Skyworks for receiving and transmitting FM stereo radio signals and other Bands.
