RDA 5807 FM Radio Tuner

The RDA5807 is a radio chip for receiving FM stereo radio signals and RDS information.

The RDA5807 radio chip is available in several variants.

It supports the following features

  • FM in range 50 - 115 MHz
  • RDS Information
  • Output Volume control
  • Digital adaptive noise cancellation
  • Bass Boost
  • Analog Line Level Output.
  • Frequency spacing 25KHz, 50KHz, 100KHz, 200KHz
  • Programmable de-emphasis (50/75 Âµs)
  • I2C Control

The RDA5807 radio chip is one of the radio chips supported by the Radio Library.

RDA5807M Module

The RDA5807 radio chip is available on small boards including the resonator and some capacitors that can easily be used by soldering wires or connector pins to it.
